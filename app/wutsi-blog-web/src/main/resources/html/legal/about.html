Turn your content to a source of income

We believe that words have value. Wutsi helps independent writers to make an income from their content, to support them to work on what they deeply value.

How the Wutsi model works

We help writers build their audience

Writers publish their stories, share them on their social media to convert their follower to readers and subscribers into their newsletter. Wutsi automatically send all publications via email to their newsletter.

We support writers via Wutsi Partner Program

Writers can join the Wutsi Partner Program and make a monthly revenu based on the trafic on their publications.

We help writers to diversify their revenus

Writers can receive online donations from their subscribers, setup an online store to sell their digital products like e-books, documents etc.

We only make money when writers make money

Setting up blog, newsletter or store is all free. When a writer receive a donation or make a sale, the revenues are shared between the writer (70%) and Wutsi (30%).
